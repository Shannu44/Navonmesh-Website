Cultural Fest Competitions

Vividha Vaibhava (Variety Show)

1. Theme: Kannada Nadu Vaibhav.
2. Only one team allowed from each college.
3. 15 minutes performance + 5 minutes for stage setup.
4. Costumes and makeup not provided by organizers.
5. Uniqueness and variety in performance are important (dance, skit, mime, yakshagana, song, etc.).
6. Exceeding time limits results in point deduction.
7. Best MC will be awarded.
8. More participants and performances will be considered in judgment.
9. Judge’s decision is final.

Natana Vaibhava (Mono Act)

1. Solo performance: one performer per college.
2. Time limit: 10 minutes.
3. Language: Kannada.
4. No harmful or illegal content.
5. Judgment based on creativity, storytelling, expressions, body language, overall impact.
6. Judge’s decision is final.

Natya Vaibhava (Bharatanatyam)

1. Solo performer per college.
2. Time limit: 10 minutes.
3. Judgment based on Thala, technique, rhythm, Abhinaya, costumes, footwork, overall impression.

Gana Vaibhava (Janapada Geetha)

1. One team per college with 4-6 members.
2. Instruments allowed; karaoke not allowed.
3. Duration: 4+1 minutes.
4. Filmy-style folk songs not allowed.

Vadana Vaibhava (Face Painting)

1. One team per college with 2 participants.
2. Duration: 60 minutes.
3. Theme: Team choice.
4. Judgment based on creativity, innovation, color, neatness.
