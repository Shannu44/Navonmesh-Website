About IMJISC

IMJISC, affiliated to Mangalore University, is a degree college established in the year 2022 in memory of the founder IM Jayaram Shetty. The institute offers Bachelor degree programmes in Commerce and Management (B.Com & BBA) and Computer Applications (BCA).These courses are reinforced by career oriented professional guidance & training and certificate courses. The academic progress of the students and their personality development are administered in tune with aspirations of National Education Policy 2020. The leadership of the institution strongly believes that knowledge alone will not help the students to make their life. Therefore, we, at our institute, along with curriculum delivery, focus on empowering the students for a better future through a number of initiatives, academic as well as non-academic.
