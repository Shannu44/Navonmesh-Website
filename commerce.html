Commerce Fest Competitions

Jnana Kaushala (Commerce Quiz)

1. Each team must consist of exactly 2 members.
2. Only one team per college.
3. Team members cannot be substituted once the competition has started.
4. Teams must register by 5th November 2024. Late registration will not be accepted under any circumstances.
5. The quiz will consist of 3 rounds which include E-Vanijya Shastra Spardha (Preliminary round), Drishya Samvaada (Semi-final round), and Commerce Crorepati (Final round).
6. Each round will have a set of questions which can be in the form of a single word answer, multiple choice, true/false, or audio-visual questions.
7. Participants must bring their phone.
8. Tiebreaker rounds will be conducted if needed.
9. Audiences are not allowed to assist or provide answers to any team.
10. Any form of cheating or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification.
11. The quiz master's decision will be final and binding.

Kala Kaushala (Business Rangoli)

1. The rangoli's size is limited to 4ft x 4ft.
2. Participants are required to use only rangoli colors and bring their own materials.
3. There is a time limit of 1 hour + 15 minutes for completing the rangoli.
4. The group can consist of exactly 4 members.
5. Judgment is based on creativity, relevance to the theme, clarity, color combination, and overall appearance.

Tantra Kaushala (Mad Ad)

1. Each team can consist of 3-6 members. Teams must register in advance.
2. Each team will get 6 minutes to perform their ad; an additional 1 minute can be allotted for setup if required. Exceeding the time limit will lead to point deductions.
3. The performance must be original and creative.
4. Humor and wit are encouraged, but no offensive language or inappropriate content is allowed.
5. Use of props and costumes is allowed but should be minimal and easily manageable.
6. The ad can be performed in English or Kannada.
7. Judgment will be based on Creativity and Originality, Humor and Entertainment Value, Clarity and Message, Teamwork and Coordination, and Adherence to Time.
8. The use of hazardous materials or objects that could damage the venue is strictly prohibited.

Anveshana (Treasure Hunt)

1. Each team must consist of 4 members: 2 from commerce and 2 from Science.
2. Participants must have participated in any one of the other events of NAVONMESH.
3. Clues must be solved in sequence to progress.
4. The hunt area is restricted to designated zones only.
5. Any damage to property or environment is prohibited.
6. The game is time-bound; all teams must return by the set deadline.
7. No use of electronic devices.
8. Tiebreaker rounds will be conducted if needed.
9. Audiences are not allowed to assist or provide answers to any team.
10. Any form of cheating or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification.
