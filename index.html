About IMJISC
IMJ Institute of Science & Commerce, Moodlakatte Kundapura is a degree college established in the year 2021 under the aegis of Moodlakatte Nagarathna Bhujanga Shetty Trust. The institute offers Bachelor degree programmes in Commerce (B.Com), Management (BBA) and Computer Applications (BCA). These courses are reinforced by career oriented professional guidance & training and certificate courses like Practical Accounting and Taxation, Data Analysis, Cloud Computing and Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The leadership of the institution strongly believes that subject knowledge alone will not help the students to build their life. Therefore, we, at our institute, along with curriculum delivery, focus on empowering the students for a better future through a number of initiatives, academic as well as non-academic.
